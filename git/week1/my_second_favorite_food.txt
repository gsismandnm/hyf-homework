Chicken

Toss the chicken breast with garlic powder, salt, and pepper. 
Heat olive oil in a 10-inch skillet over medium heat. 
Add chicken breast and cook until browned, stirring often, about 3 minutes. 
Add garlic to skillet and cook for 30 seconds more.
Stir in rice and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, and cover. 
Cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Remove pan from heat and place baby spinach on top of the rice. Cover and let sit for 5 minutes.
Remove lid and stir well. Stir in cream and serve.
